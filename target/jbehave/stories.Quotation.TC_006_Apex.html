Scenario: TC_006_Apex 001 Apex Test Case Marine - Marine Cargo - Cargo Policy (Saudi Riyals)

Given I navigate to 'http://hq-opt-ins-21:9090/ordsapexqa/r/qaapex/aman/login'
And I login as 'aman_opt' with password 'aman_opt'
And I wait for '5' sec
When I click on 'QuotationCardDashboard'
And Verify 'QuotationsTitle' equals 'Quotations' text
And I click on 'JeddahClaimCenterCard'
And I click on 'NewQuotationButton'
And I click on 'MarineCard'
And I click on 'MarineCargoCard'
And I click on 'CargoPolicyCard'
And Verify 'QutClassOfBusinessDisplay' equals '10 - Marine Cargo' text
And Verify 'QutPolicyTypeDisplay' equals '10 - Cargo Policy' text
!-- Fill the mandatory Quotation Details
And Select '1-Direct' value from 'QutSourceCombobox' dropdown list
And Verify 'QutPaymentTermCombobox' contains '2-Account' text
And Verify 'QutQuotDate' value equals sysdate
And Verify 'QutQuotValidityDate' value equals sysdate plus '1' months minus one day
And Set 'QutInsStDateInput' value to 'sysdateformatted'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I click on 'QutInsStDateInput'
And I press Enter on 'QutInsStDateInput'
And I wait for '5' sec
And Verify 'QutInsEdDateInput' input is empty
And I scroll to 'QutCalculationBasisCombobox'
And I wait for '5' sec
And Select '1 - Pro Rata' value from 'QutCalculationBasisCombobox' dropdown list
And Select '1 - Saudi Riyals' value from 'QutCrCdCombobox' dropdown list
And Verify 'QutUnitPriceVal' equals '1' text
And I scroll to 'CreateButton'
And I click on 'CreateButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I scroll to 'SuccessfullyAlert'
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Data Saved Successfully.' text
And I get text from 'QutQuotNoCreateText' and save it as 'QuotationNo'
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Fill in Quotation Value Types
And I click on 'EditButtonQuotationValue'
And I wait for '5' sec
And Verify IG 'calc_ig' row '20 - LC invoice value' Local/Amount can be filled
And I set IG 'calc_ig' from first row '20 - LC invoice value' to random numbers between '1' and '50' for all enabled cells excluding DDL
And I wait for '5' sec
And I click on 'SaveButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I scroll to top of page
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Fill in Quotation Client
And Select '1093 - Staff / Ammar Ahmed Hamza Ghor' value from 'QurQutClientCombobox' dropdown list
And Select '1093 - Staff / Ammar Ahmed Hamza Ghor' value from 'QurQutClientNoCombobox' dropdown list
And I click on 'ApplyChangesButton'
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Data Updated Successfully.' text
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Last Page
And Select '1-AWS (Sea)' value from 'QutCoverGroupCombobox' dropdown list
And Select '2 - Cheque' value from 'QutCollectionTypeCombobox' dropdown list
And I scroll to end of page
And I click on 'ApplyChangesButton'
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Data Updated Successfully.' text
And Verify text of 'QutQuotNoFinalPageText' contains saved value 'QuotationNo'
And I scroll to 'DeleteButton'
And I click on 'DeleteButton'
And I click on 'DeleteButtonPopup'
And I wait for '5' sec
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Quotation Deleted Successfully!' text
!-- Logout
And I wait for '7' sec
Then I click on 'UserDDL'
And I click on 'LogoutButton'
And Verify 'loginButton' is present
