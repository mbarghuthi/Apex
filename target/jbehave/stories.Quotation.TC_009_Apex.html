And Verify 'QuotationsTitle' equals 'Quotations' text
And I click on 'JeddahClaimCenterCard'
And I click on 'NewQuotationButton'

And I click on 'MarineHullCard'

And I scroll to 'QutCalculationBasisCombobox'
And I wait for '5' sec

And Verify 'QutUnitPriceVal' equals '1' text
And I scroll to 'CreateButton'
And I click on 'CreateButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I scroll to 'SuccessfullyAlert'
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Data Saved Successfully.' text
And I get text from 'QutQuotNoCreateText' and save it as 'QuotationNo'
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Fill the mandatory Risk Details

And I switch to iframe 'AddNewRiskIframe'
And Select '1 - Risk Basis' value from 'BreakdownCombobox' dropdown list
And I click on 'ApplyChangesButtonIframe'
And I switch back to main content
And I wait for '5' sec
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I scroll to 'EditButton'
And I click on 'EditButton'
And Verify IG 'RiskCalculation_ig' row '17 - Marine Sum Insured' Local/Amount can be filled
And I set SECOND IG 'RiskCalculation_ig' from first row '17 - Marine Sum Insured' to random numbers between '1' and '50' for all enabled cells excluding DDL
And I wait for '5' sec
And I click on 'SaveButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I scroll to top of page
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Fill in Quotation Value Types
And I click on 'EditButtonQuotationValue'
And I wait for '5' sec
And Verify IG 'calc_ig' row '26 - Overage Premium' Local/Amount can be filled
And I set IG 'calc_ig' from first row '26 - Overage Premium' to random numbers between '1' and '50' for all enabled cells excluding DDL
And I click on 'SaveButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
And I scroll to top of page
And I wait for '5' sec
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Fill in Quotation Client
And Select '1093 - Staff / Ammar Ahmed Hamza Ghor' value from 'QurQutClientCombobox' dropdown list
And Select '1093 - Staff / Ammar Ahmed Hamza Ghor' value from 'QurQutClientNoCombobox' dropdown list
And I click on 'ApplyChangesButton'
And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Data Updated Successfully.' text
And I click on 'NextButton'
And I wait for '5' sec
!-- Last Page

And Select '2 - Cheque' value from 'QutCollectionTypeCombobox' dropdown list
And I scroll to end of page
And I click on 'ApplyChangesButton'

And Verify text of 'QutQuotNoFinalPageText' contains saved value 'QuotationNo'
And I scroll to 'DeleteButton'
And I click on 'DeleteButton'
And I click on 'DeleteButtonPopup'

And Verify 'SuccessfullyAlert' contains 'Quotation Deleted Successfully!' text
